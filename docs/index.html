Built different

Most voice AI is a chatbot on a phone line. Tavin is something else.

🎯

Task-driven, not chat-driven

Tavin doesn't make small talk. Every call has a goal: confirm an appointment, offer a reschedule, fill a cancellation. The conversation follows a structured path with a clear outcome. When the task is done, the call is done.

🔒

Private by design

Your client data never leaves infrastructure we control. No cloud AI services process your calls. Speech recognition, language understanding, and voice generation all run on dedicated hardware — not rented from a third party.

🧠

Listen → Understand → Speak

Every call flows through a three-stage pipeline: speech-to-text captures what the client says, a language model understands what they mean and decides what to do, then text-to-speech responds in a natural voice. Each stage is optimized for short, purposeful conversations — not open-ended chat.

⚡

Fast where it matters

Common responses are pre-rendered before the call even starts. When a client says "yes," Tavin responds instantly — no waiting for a model to generate audio. Novel responses (names, dates, rescheduling offers) are generated live, but the most frequent parts of the conversation feel immediate.

The difference between a chatbot and a task agent is the difference between a conversation that wanders and a conversation that gets something done. Tavin knows the appointment, knows the schedule, knows the client's language — and uses all of that to finish the job in under two minutes.
